# Glossary

## Predictive Strategy Forecasting
System that estimates strategy impact before simulation and execution.

## Expected rank delta
Predicted ranking movement from executing a strategy.

## Expected traffic delta
Predicted traffic movement from strategy + context.

## Confidence score
Estimated reliability of the forecast.

## Risk score
Estimated downside probability and severity.

## Industry priors
Segment-specific baseline expectations from industry models.

## Graph evidence features
Inputs derived from Global Learning Graph support and confidence.
