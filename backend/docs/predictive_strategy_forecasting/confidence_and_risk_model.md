# Confidence and Risk Model

## Confidence scoring
Confidence estimates probability that predicted impact is reliable.

## Confidence signals
- support count
- variance
- industry fit
- evidence recency
- calibration residuals

## Risk scoring
Risk estimates downside probability and expected severity.

## Operational use
- Confidence/risk thresholds gate autonomous execution.
- High-risk strategies require stronger simulation confirmation.
- Post-outcome calibration updates confidence and risk tables.
